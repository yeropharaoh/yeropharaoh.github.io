Yieldr blog site, created using Ghost (a publishing/CMS platform)

Built from the ground up with one other colleague, the Yieldr blog site was built with Ghost to make it easy for our content team to write and publish articles. We created our own template and built the website according to design. This includes pagination for the articles, author pages, similar post recommendation, a subscribe page/cta and article search functionality. The blog played a crucial role in positioing Yieldr as a thought-leader and garnering interest from airline industry professionals (almost 6000 in less than a year!)

Stack: ghost(cms), handlebars, zurb foundation, sass, jQuery, node.js
