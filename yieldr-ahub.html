The Airline Hub: a collection of the world's airlines and the technologies they use

As a more experimental third project, I was tasked together with another colleague to create the airline hub as a means to get more interest from airlines. The website's ambition was to provide aesthetically-pleasing information on all of the world's airlines. We first built the site in VueJS and later in Angular 7. A Google Sheet was created for the marketing team to fill in/update airline data. The sheet data was converted into JSON and the airline-tail SVG's placed in the sheet as links from our CDN. The airlines are sorted alphabetically and divided into low-cost and full-service airlines. Furthermore, users can search for airline name, technology, or country of airlines.

Stack: angular 7, ngx-pagination, zurb foundation, sass, nodejs.
