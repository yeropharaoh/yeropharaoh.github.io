The Airline Hub: a collection of the world's airlines and the technologies they use

As a more experimental third project, a colleague and I first built the airline hub in Vue before building it out fully in Angular. Since the website wanted to have/update information of many of the world's airlines, a Google Sheet was created for the marketing team to fill in/update airline data. The sheet data was converted into JSON and and API helped us fetch and display the data. The airlines are sorted alphabetically and divided into low-cost and full-service airlines. Users are able to search for airlines by name, technology and country. All three Yieldr websites helped position Yieldr as a thought leader in the aviation-tech space with over 290 content and product signups in about 6 weeks.

Stack: angular 7, ngx-pagination, google sheet api, zurb foundation, sass, nodejs.
